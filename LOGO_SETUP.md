# LOGO SETUP GUIDE

## Adding Your BESTWAY Consulting Logo

Your website has been updated to display the logo in **3 prominent locations**:

### 📍 Logo Placement Locations:

1. **Navigation Bar** (Top-left) - Sticky navigation
2. **Hero Section** (Home page center) - With rotating animation and glow effect
3. **Footer** (Bottom) - Branded footer section with company name

### 🖼️ How to Add Your Logo:

1. **Prepare Your Logo Image:**
   - Recommended size: 256x256 pixels or larger (PNG with transparency works best)
   - Format: PNG, JPG, or WebP
   - The image will be automatically resized to fit each section

2. **Save the Logo File:**
   - Save your logo as `logo.png` in the `public/` folder
   - File path: `public/logo.png`
   - The public folder is already created and ready

3. **File Structure:**
   ```
   Website/
   ├── public/
   │   └── logo.png  ← Place your logo here
   ├── app/
   ├── components/
   └── ...
   ```
